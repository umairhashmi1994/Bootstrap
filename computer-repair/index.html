Who We Are

Stallion Computers provides premium customer service and technical support for all customers in the Illawarra.

Our highly qualified team share a passion for technology with skill, knowledge and experience as our core fundamentals to provide the highest standard in technical support. Our team hold certifications from reputable brands such as Apple, Microsoft, Bitdefender and much more. Our Senior Technical Manager has over 15 years experience and has worked for top tech companies such as IBM, Apple & Microsoft. We believe in providing the highest standards in technical services to ensure customer satisfaction.
